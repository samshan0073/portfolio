About Me

I’m a Game Design major passionate about engaging gameplay systems, with a particular love for JRPGs, ARPGs, and Roguelikes. My focus is on game mechanics design, and I’m continually working to improve my 3D modeling skills to expand my creative abilities. Outside of game design, I enjoy J-Pop, animation, city walks, badminton, Manga, and mystery novels.

Projects

2D Platformer (IGME-106)

A 2D platformer where players navigate challenging platforms to collect apples and reach the final goal within a limited time, testing precision and timing skills.

Pokemon Finder (IGME-235)

A web application that allows users to search for Pokémon using filters or by name. It provides basic information about each Pokémon and links to its official Pokémon Wiki page for more details.

Grid Clicker Webgame (IGME-235)

A fast-paced web game built with JavaScript where players click on red grids as quickly as possible to score points. The game challenges reflexes and hand-eye coordination.

Planet (IGME-202)

A bullet-dodge game where players use satellites orbiting Earth to eliminate incoming meteors. It features a variety of steering behaviors for strategic gameplay.

Weird Assets Production (IGME-219)

A collection of unique, mythical-themed 3D assets and a Maze-style level design.

Space Apes (IGME-220)

A strategic board game where players control apes competing to develop technology while hindering opponents. The ultimate goal is to achieve space travel first, combining strategy and planning.
